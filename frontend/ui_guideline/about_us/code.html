Engineering the Next Generation

The premier engineering outreach organization at the University of Toronto, dedicated to inspiring high school students through innovation and mentorship.

Our Mission

HiSkule serves as the bridge between the rigorous academic world of University of Toronto Engineering and the vibrant, curious minds of high school students. We believe that engineering is more than just a degree—it's a mindset of problem-solving that should be accessible to everyone.

Democratizing technical education across Ontario.

Fostering a diverse community of future innovators.

Connecting secondary students with world-class faculty.

A Century of Impact

1904

The Founding

Founded as the Engineering Society's primary outreach arm to connect the university with local Toronto schools.

1985

Modern Expansion

Launch of the first provincial design competitions, reaching students from Windsor to Ottawa.

2024

Digital Frontier

Reaching over 5,000 students annually through hybrid workshops and global mentorship platforms.
